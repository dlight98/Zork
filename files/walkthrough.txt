Welcome to Hyrule. This dungeon features many clever puzzles and enemies.
To progress through each room, you must either solve a puzzle or defeat
some monsters. The puzzles have varying elements, like wind and electricity,
and the mosnters have many different fighting styles, like Psychic and Fighting.
Your ultimate goal is to defeat the final boss at the end of the dungeon.
Once you have done so, you will be declared the winner.
